## Current Project Vision

An AI-powered Quran learning and study companion focused on helping users understand, retain, and build consistent study habits, rather than simply reading the Quran or chatting with an AI.

## Core Problem:
The project addresses the fact that many people can read the Quran but struggle with:

Understanding verses
Learning Quranic Arabic vocabulary
Connecting themes and related verses
Retaining what they've learned
Staying consistent in their study routine

## Main Features:
From the document, the planned features include:

Interactive verse-by-verse explanations and background
AI assistant using RAG with trusted sources
Arabic vocabulary learning (meaning, root word, occurrences)
AI-generated summaries
Quizzes after study sessions
Vocabulary quizzes
Daily streaks, points, and gamification
Progress tracking
Reflection/journaling (tentative)
Chatbot for topic-specific questions (tentative)

## User Flow:
The intended experience is:

Select a Surah
Read an Ayah
Tap a verse or word for contextual information
Ask AI questions grounded in authenticated sources
Receive summaries, quizzes, and vocabulary practice
Track learning progress through streaks and performance

## Technical Direction
The AI stack currently envisioned includes:

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
LLM-powered explanations and quizzes
Semantic search
Vector database
Natural language Q&A grounded in trusted references
Elevator Pitch

The project is positioned as an AI-powered Quran learning companion that complements traditional learning through authenticated sources, personalized learning, and habit-building—not as a replacement for scholars or traditional study.
